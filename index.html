Mission

To support all Ontario equestrians involved at all levels of participation including grassroots participants (new riders, free riders, riders in lessons), competitive and developmental high performance riders have access to high quality programs, certified coaches, safe and well governed facilities, competition with certified judges, stewards and course designers.

Vision

The Ontario Equestrian Federation is the Ontario leader in innovative rider-centred programming and governance supporting a variety of participant pathways for riders of all ages with their equine partner.
