Upcoming Events

JUN

29

Annual General Meeting

Caledon Equestrian Park, 200 Pine Avenue, Palgrave, ON, L7E 0M1 @ 7:30 pm.

The AGM provides members with the opportunity to learn about the progress made by the OEF staff and board over the year, the plans for the next year and the opportunity for members to express concerns and/or ask questions of their federation. The annual OEF Board of Directors election also takes place during the AGM. All individual adult members in good standing are eligible to stand for election and vote.

Horse Day

For more information or media inquiries about Ontario Horse Day, please contact Brandon Hall at b.hall@horse.on.ca or 1-877-441-7112 ext. 30

Horse Day is a FREE event for everyone! With over 100 OEF accredited riding facilities across Ontario, find a Horse Day location that’s close to you to come out and celebrate horses and Equestrian sport.

All Breeds Equine Expo

Caledon Equestrian Park, 200 Pine Avenue, Palgrave, ON, L7E 0M1 @ 4pm. pm.
